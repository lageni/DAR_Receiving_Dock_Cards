BIG QUERY SYNC QUERY:

The exact query used for missing date sync:

SELECT * FROM `wmt-ambient-centeng.6068_Engineering.ACL_READ_RATE`
WHERE PICK_TYPE_CODE NOT IN ('DPAL', 'LBSS')
AND ACL_INSERT_DATE IN {"2026-01-01", "2026-01-02", ...}

- Filters OUT: DPAL and LBSS pick types (as specified)
- Filters IN: Only missing dates (generated from local DB)
- Fetches: All columns from ACL_READ_RATE table
- Appends: Results to SQLite database
