These are the INVENTORY API credentials (different from MDM API).

UPDATE YOUR .env FILE:
======================

Edit .env and replace the placeholder values:

  INVENTORY_JWT_TOKEN=your-jwt-token-here
  INVENTORY_USER_ID=your-user-id-here
  INVENTORY_API_URL=https://inventory-viewer.prod.walmart.net
  INVENTORY_DEFAULT_NODE=6068
  INVENTORY_COUNTRY_CODE=US

Get your JWT_TOKEN and USER_ID from your Walmart account credentials.
